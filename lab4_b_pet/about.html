My name is Agnesa Suli, and just a few days ago, I turned 20. I am currently a student at the Faculty of Computer Engineering and Computer Science, at the University of "Ss. Cyril and Methodius" in Skopje, majoring in Computer Science.
I consider myself a colorful personality — I don’t know if it’s because I’m a Gemini or simply because I still haven’t found my full self among all the possibilities life offers us today. I enjoy challenges (evident by the fact that I study at FINKI), I love adventures, photography, and literature — but I’ll talk more about that in the Hobbies section.
When it comes to computer science, I’ve always been curious about how things work — how to build them, how to improve them, and how to make them serve a purpose.
And finally, to answer the frequently asked question: "Will AI replace humans?" Personally, I believe that AI will actually give us — programmers and professionals of all kinds — more time. Time to think, to analyze, and to solve the core problems of our fields, while AI takes care of the writing, scripting, and unnecessary micro-details.
I don’t believe in competition — and I don’t think humans should either — because as long as we were capable of creating AI, we are capable of growing beyond it.
